The Saturn Shack

About Us

In 2000, TBC launched as a unique space-themed café, serving premium American ice cream, cakes, and desserts with a cosmic twist. Not long after, a stellar trattoria-inspired menu was introduced, expanding into an extensive Italian selection that quickly became a favorite among visitors. The restaurant's success is built on a vast variety of dishes and its unwavering commitment to consistently high standards, ensuring an out-of-this-world dining experience.

The Saturn Shack offers a unique space-themed dining experience, complete with cosmic décor and immersive ambiance. Enjoy a diverse menu of American ice cream, galaxy-inspired desserts, and Italian trattoria favorites, all made with high-quality ingredients. We provide intergalactic catering for events, a drinks bar featuring space-inspired cocktails, and a fun kids’ menu. Our themed events, private lunar room rentals, and exclusive space-themed merchandise make every visit memorable. For added convenience, we also offer delivery and an interactive digital menu with augmented reality features to enhance your dining journey.
